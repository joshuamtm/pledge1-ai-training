PTCF Example: Grant Report

OK Prompt: "Write a grant report"

Problem: Too vague, no context, unclear format

BEST PTCF Prompt:

"Act as a grant writing specialist for nonprofits (P). Create a quarterly impact report for our youth mentorship program (T). We served 150 at-risk youth this quarter with 85% showing improved academic performance (C). Format as a 2-page executive summary with bullet points for key metrics (F). Ask me 3 clarifying questions before you begin."

Vibe Coding: The Art of AI Collaboration

What is Vibe Coding?

The iterative process of refining prompts and working WITH AI to achieve your desired output

How We Built This Presentation

‚Ä¢ Start: "Create a slide deck about AI"

‚Ä¢ Refine: "Add COMPAS framework, make it interactive"

‚Ä¢ Iterate: "Fix the logo positioning, split long slides"

‚Ä¢ Polish: "Add both logos, ensure no scrolling"

Result: 26 polished slides in 45 minutes!

The Vibe Coding Process

  • Start with a basic prompt
  • Review the output critically
  • Provide specific feedback
  • Iterate until it matches your vision
  • Think of AI as a collaborative partner
