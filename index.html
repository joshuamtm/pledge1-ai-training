C - Context: Comprehensive Information Gathering

Providing AI with the Full Picture

What is Context?

Context is ALL relevant information about your situation:

  • Who you are and your role
  • Your organization's mission and structure
  • The current situation and challenges
  • Available resources and constraints

How to Provide Context to AI

  • Documents: Strategic plans, reports, templates
  • Transcripts: Meeting notes, interviews, recordings
  • Data: Metrics, survey results, analytics
  • Background: Industry trends, stakeholder expectations

🎯 Remember: The more comprehensive context you provide, the more accurate and relevant the AI's response will be.

Method is HOW you'll achieve your objective. Consider:

Context: Sample Discovery Prompt

Use this prompt to uncover your organization's AI landscape:

"I'm the [your role] at [organization type]. Help me conduct an AI readiness assessment. Our team of [size] currently struggles with [top 3 pain points]. We have [tech stack/tools], and our data privacy requirements include [compliance needs]. What questions should I ask my team to understand: 1) Current unofficial AI use, 2) Biggest time-wasters that AI could address, 3) Security/compliance gaps we need to fill? Please provide a 5-minute team survey template and a simple scoring rubric to assess our AI readiness level."

Why This Works

• Provides organizational context upfront

• Asks for specific deliverables (survey + rubric)

• Focuses on discovery, not solutions

Business Case Builder for Social Impact

A Social Impact Leader's Challenge

"How do I protect my programs when budgets are being cut?"

*Note: "Builder" here refers to Pledge 1% program participants

Use this prompt to clarify your true objective:

"Act as a McKinsey consultant specializing in corporate social impact ROI. I lead social impact at [company] with [# employees]. Our programs include [list programs]. Leadership is considering budget cuts. Create a comprehensive business case showing: 1) Employee retention value (cost to replace vs. engagement scores), 2) Brand value metrics (NPS correlation, PR value), 3) Customer acquisition/loyalty impact, 4) Risk mitigation value. Use industry benchmarks and format for C-suite presentation with clear financial projections."

Real Results

• Quantifies impact in $ terms executives understand

• Links to business KPIs (retention, NPS, revenue)

• Creates board-ready visuals in minutes

Vibe Coding: The Art of AI Collaboration

What is Vibe Coding?

The iterative process of refining prompts and working WITH AI to achieve your desired output

How We Built This Presentation

• Start: "Create a slide deck about AI"

• Refine: "Add COMPAS framework, make it interactive"

• Iterate: "Fix the logo positioning, split long slides"

• Polish: "Add both logos, ensure no scrolling"

Result: 26 polished slides in 45 minutes!

The Vibe Coding Process

  • Start with a basic prompt
  • Review the output critically
  • Provide specific feedback
  • Iterate until it matches your vision
  • Think of AI as a collaborative partner

S - Share: Foster Learning Culture

Multiply Impact Through Collaboration

What is Share?

Sharing accelerates everyone's learning:

  • 🤝 Get Feedback: Learn from others' experiences

When and How to Share

  • During Context: "Who else has faced this challenge?"
  • During Assessment: "Here's what worked/didn't work"
