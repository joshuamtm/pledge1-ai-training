C - Context: Comprehensive Information Gathering

Providing AI with the Full Picture

What is Context?

Context is ALL relevant information about your situation:

  • Who you are and your role
  • Your organization's mission and structure
  • The current situation and challenges
  • Available resources and constraints

How to Provide Context to AI

  • Documents: Strategic plans, reports, templates
  • Transcripts: Meeting notes, interviews, recordings
  • Data: Metrics, survey results, analytics
  • Background: Industry trends, stakeholder expectations

🎯 Remember: The more comprehensive context you provide, the more accurate and relevant the AI's response will be.

Method Deep Dive: Business Case Building

Maria's Challenge: Defending Budget to Leadership

Scenario 3: Building a Business Case

"CMO is asking for 20% budget cuts. How do I protect our social impact programs?"

Maria's Business Case Prompt:

"Act as a McKinsey consultant specializing in corporate social impact ROI. I'm Maria at TechForward (800 employees, B2B software). Our social impact programs include 10 nonprofit grants ($1.5M annually) and employee volunteer time. Leadership wants 20% cuts. Create a business case showing: 1) Employee retention value (our turnover dropped 15% since launching programs) 2) Talent acquisition benefits (42% of new hires cite our social impact) 3) Customer win rate improvement (8% higher with values-aligned prospects) 4) PR/brand value equivalent Format as executive summary with financial projections and risk analysis of cutting programs."

Maria's Results

• Quantified $3.2M annual value from programs

• Protected full budget with data-driven argument

• Got 10% increase for employee engagement initiatives

Method Deep Dive: CRAFT Framework Application

CRAFT in Action: Maria's Board Presentation

OK Prompt: "Summarize our impact data for the board"

Problem: Too vague, no context about what board cares about

Maria's EXPERT CRAFT Prompt:

"I'm preparing for TechForward's quarterly board meeting tomorrow (C). Act as a senior impact strategist with Fortune 500 board experience (R). Transform our Q3 grantee data into insights that connect social programs to our B2B software company's growth metrics (A). Create 5 crisp bullet points with supporting data, plus one compelling grantee story (F). The board includes tech industry veterans who value efficiency and ROI; they're skeptical of 'soft' benefits (T)."

🎯 Result: Board approved program expansion based on Maria's data-driven narrative

The iterative process of refining prompts and working WITH AI to achieve your desired output

P - Performance: Personal Implementation

Making Your Method Real

What is Performance?

Performance is implementing your chosen method in your daily work:

  • 🚀 Deploy: Start using your AI Package
  • 📄 Document: Keep notes on what works
  • ✅ Execute: Run through real scenarios
  • 🔄 Refine: Adjust prompts as you learn

Key Performance Activities for Individual Users

  • Start Small: Test with low-stakes tasks first
  • Track Time: Measure hours saved
  • Note Improvements: Document quality gains
  • Stay Flexible: Adapt to what works for you

🎯 Remember: You're optimizing for YOUR workflow, not anyone else's!

Measuring Your Success

What is Assessment?
